# Returns & Refunds Policy

- Unworn items with original tags can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.
- Items still "processing" (not yet shipped) can be cancelled any time for a full refund.
- Sale and clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned unless faulty.
- Worn or washed items are not eligible for a refund but may qualify for the warranty/repairs program.
- To start a return, use the "Returns" link on your account page, or ask support to open one for you.
- Exchanges for a different size of the same item ship free of charge.
