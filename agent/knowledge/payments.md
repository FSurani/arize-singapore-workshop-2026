# Payments & Billing

- We accept all major credit cards, PayNow, and Atome installments.
- Payment is captured when your order ships, not when it is placed.
- Atome splits your purchase into 3 interest-free monthly payments.
- Prices are shown in Singapore Dollars (SGD) and include GST.
- We do not store full card numbers; payments are processed by our PCI-compliant provider.
- For billing disputes or duplicate charges, escalate to a human agent so we can investigate the transaction.
