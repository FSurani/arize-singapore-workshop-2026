# Warranty & Repairs

- All Sunrise Outfitters gear carries a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects.
- Defects include failed seams, broken zippers, and delamination of waterproof membranes.
- Normal wear and tear, accidental damage, and improper care are not covered.
- For a warranty claim, email a photo of the issue and your order number to support; no receipt needed if the order is in our system.
- Approved claims are repaired free, or replaced if repair is not possible.
- Out-of-warranty repairs are offered at a flat S$25 plus return shipping.
- Turnaround for repairs is typically 2-3 weeks.
