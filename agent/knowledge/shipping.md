# Shipping Policy

- Standard shipping within Singapore takes 2-4 business days.
- International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on destination.
- Orders are dispatched from our Singapore warehouse Monday-Friday.
- A tracking number is emailed to you as soon as your order ships.
- Standard shipping is free for orders over S$80; otherwise a flat S$5 applies.
- Express shipping (next business day, Singapore only) is available for S$15 at checkout.
- We currently do not ship to P.O. boxes for orders containing footwear.
